10* Household Lifehacks you'll wish you knew sooner

Please note these are not universal and results may vary depending on which product you have

These everyday tips to make household and daily life just that little bit easier.

  1. First off on the list, did you know that using ice cubes in your dryer helps prevent wrinkles in your clothes?
  2. You can use essential oils to polish your wooden furniture if you're out of polish.
  3. Scuff marks on your hardwood floor? Rub them with a clean tennis ball!
  4. If you still have cassette cases lying around, you can use them as an improvised phone holder.
  5. When you have a hole in your tire but can't pinpoint where, cover it with dish soap and water, and bubbles will form where the puncture is.
  6. Steam clean your microwave by putting a bowl of water inside and microwaving it for 2-3 minutes. The dried bits of food should be easier to remove.
  7. Frozen grapes can help chill your glass of wine without diluting it!
  8. When cutting onions, put a piece of warmed onion where you would rest a pen to prevent teary eyes. This one was taught to me by my mother.
  9. Dry tea bags or newspaper can be put inside shoes to help eliminate bad smell.
  10. For people with broken keyboard stands, binder clips do a good job of replacing them.
  11. Spaghetti is very effective for lighting candles in jars, which would otherwise be hard to reach with matches. This last one is on me.
